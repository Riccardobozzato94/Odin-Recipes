Pizza

Description

Pizza is a popular Italian dish that has become a global favorite. It typically consists of a flat, round base of dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese (most commonly mozzarella), and various toppings such as pepperoni, vegetables, mushrooms, and more. Pizza is baked in an oven and can come in many varieties, including thin-crust, deep-dish, and wood-fired styles. Each region in Italy, and around the world, has its own unique take on pizza.

Ingredient

  • Dough: Made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and sometimes olive oil.
  • Tomato Sauce: Often seasoned with garlic, basil, oregano, and other herbs
  • Cheese: Mozzarella is the most common, but other cheeses like Parmesan, cheddar, or gorgonzola can also be used.
  • Toppings: These can vary widely but often include pepperoni, sausage, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers, olives, anchovies, and more.
  • Olive Oil: Often drizzled on top or used in the dough.
